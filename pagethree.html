Cacio E Pepe Recipe

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Unsalted butter
  • Cracked black pepper
  • Parmesean cheese
  • Pecorino

Steps

  1. Cook pasta
  2. Melt the unsalted butter and add the pepper. Cook while swirling in a pan for about 1 minutes
  3. Add sauce, parmesesan cheese, and the pecorino to the pasta
