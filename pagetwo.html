Caesar Salad Recipe

Want a heart healthy salad to go with your delicious pesto pasta? Check out this recipe for our delicious Caesar salad!

Ingredients

  • Romaine lettuce
  • Shaved parmesean cheese
  • Croutons
  • Caesar salad dressing

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients into a large mixing bowl
  2. Add the Caesar salad dressing to your liking
  3. Enjoy!
